        How to Play Scoundrel

        Goal: Clear the dungeon deck without losing all 20 health.

        Deck

        A 44-card deck: 26 Monsters (♣/♠ 2–14), 9 Weapons (♦ 2–10), 9 Potions (♥ 2–10).

        Each Room

        1. Draw cards until 4 are visible.
        2. Choose to Avoid (place all 4 on the bottom in order — cannot avoid twice in a row) or Face the room.
        3. If you Face it, resolve 3 of 4 cards in any order. The 4th carries to the next room.

        Cards

        • Weapon ♦: equip it. Discards your previous weapon and any monsters stacked on it.
        • Potion ♥: heal by its value (max 20). Only the first potion in a room heals; later potions are wasted.
        • Monster ♣/♠: fight bare-handed (take full damage), or with your weapon.

        Weapon Rule (non-increasing)

        Your weapon can only kill monsters in non-increasing order. After defeating a monster of value N, you can only use the weapon on monsters of value ≤ N. Otherwise you must fight bare-handed.

        Example: Equip ♦6. Defeat ♣10 (damage 10−6=4). Next monster ♠9 is too big (9 > 10? no — 9 ≤ 10, OK). After ♣10 stack, you can fight ≤10. After ♠9, only ≤9. And so on.

        Scoring

        • Win (deck cleared): score = remaining health.
        • Loss (health ≤ 0): score = −(sum of monster values left in the deck).
